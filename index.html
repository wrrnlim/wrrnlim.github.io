Achievements

Below are a few of my achievements:

Edmonton Public Schools Esports (Formerly McNally Esports League)

A few of my friends and I founded the McNally Esports League (MESL) in hopes that we could bring competitive video game tournaments into the school environment, and show that competitive video games have a lot of the same qualities as traditional sports, such as teamwork, communication, and strategy. We hosted several tournaments at McNally High School, with our biggest tournament, the McNally Invitational, seeing participation from 7 Edmonton Public schools. We had received a grant from Young Edmonton Grants, as well as sponsorships from several local businesses. The tournament also received media coverage from Global News, CBC News, CTV News, and CBC radio. After my departure at McNally High School, the MESL was transformed into a school board wide league, and was renamed as Edmonton Public Schools Esports.

Repeat Badminton Medalist

Obtained medals in multiple badminton tournaments, including Alberta School's Althetic Association Zone tournaments, Edmonton District Badminton Association tournaments, and Concordia Open.

Edmonton Chinese Bilingual Education Association Yearly Scholarship Winner

Scholarship winner from 2013-2019.

Extended Essay on Big Data

As part of the full International Baccalaureate program, I wrote a 4000 word extended essay on big data and the ethical concerns it may raise in a global society.
